MAGRS AI Governance Readiness Assessment

This short assessment helps organizations reflect on their awareness of artificial intelligence use in everyday work.

1. What type of organization are you?
2. Approximately how many employees work in your organization?
3. Are employees using AI tools such as ChatGPT or Copilot?
4. Has leadership discussed AI use within the organization?
5. Does your organization have guidance for AI-assisted work?
6. Who is responsible for work produced with AI assistance?
7. Would you like to receive your readiness results?
