Participate in MAGRS

The Marshall AI Governance Readiness Standard (MAGRS) provides a simple, structured approach to maintaining accountability as artificial intelligence becomes part of everyday work.

What Participation Includes

  • Initial readiness review
  • Access to the MAGRS framework
  • Quarterly readiness reporting
  • Active governance credential

Participation Model

Organizations participate in MAGRS through an annual cycle of governance awareness and reporting.

  • Initial Readiness Review: $85
  • Annual Participation: $295

Quarterly Responsibility

Participating organizations submit a brief quarterly readiness report to maintain an active credential.

Failure to maintain reporting will result in suspension of active status until reporting is brought current.
